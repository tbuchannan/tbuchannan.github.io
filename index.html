Eggs and Bacon Breakfast

Description

A classic breakfast, cant go wrong with eggs, bacon and random green stuff on your plate.

Directions

  1. Heat 2 pans or large skillets, place roughly one tablespoon of butter and allow it to melt and spread along surface.
  2. Open bacon, and place desired slices evenly spread out on top of a pan
  3. Flip periodically so they can cook evenly on both sides, remove when they begin to brown
  4. Crack desired amount of eggs into a clean bowl and mix
  5. Add some milk into the mixture of eggs
  6. Add some butter into the beaten eggs
  7. Pour beaten eggs onto pan, skillet, mix eggs as they begin to solidfy
  8. Mix eggs around evenly so they can cook all the way through
  9. Remove eggs after they begin to brown
  10. Clean pans, or rinse and put into dishwasher
  11. Add salt and pepper to eggs
  12. Enjoy!
